# uCase

uCase lets you **customize your phone case** to match your personality and vibe completely.

With uCase, you’re the artist — throw in your favorite picture, emojis, TV shows, anime, sports team, pet, family, or wedding! Anything really. **Your phone case, your rules.**

## Features

- Upload your own images or designs
- Add emojis, icons, and other personal touches
- Preview your phone case before ordering
- User-friendly interface to select your phone model

## How to Use

1. **Pick your phone model**  
   Select your phone model from our easy list. Let’s find the perfect fit!

2. **Create your design**  
   Add images, emojis, or any personal elements you like.

3. **Preview & Submit**  
   See your custom phone case in action and place your request.
